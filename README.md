# China Claw

Research and introduce China-based alternatives, counterparts, or "平替" products to OpenClaw, organized by company and product positioning.

## What it does
- Searches for Chinese AI agent platforms, workflow automation tools, and multi-agent systems
- Groups findings by company rather than by scattered web mentions
- Compares each product along positioning, target users, core capabilities, and similarity to OpenClaw
- Delivers market overviews, shortlist recommendations, or company-by-company scans as needed

## Example scenarios

**1. Find the best Chinese alternatives to OpenClaw**
> 👤 "What are the best Chinese alternatives to OpenClaw?"
> 🤖 A market overview listing companies and their products, comparing each to OpenClaw along axes of positioning, target users, core capabilities, and similarity level (close/partial/adjacent substitute).

**2. Get a shortlist recommendation for a team**
> 👤 "I need an AI agent platform for my team, recommend one from Chinese companies"
> 🤖 A shortlist of 3-5 most relevant products with adaptation rationale, a clear recommendation, and reasons why other options were not recommended.

**3. Scan specific companies for OpenClaw-like products**
> 👤 "Does Alibaba or ByteDance have an OpenClaw-like product?"
> 🤖 A company-by-company scan showing each named company's relevant product, overlap with OpenClaw, target audience (enterprise/developer/consumer), and current maturity assessment.
